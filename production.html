Production

Looks like you went farther into the site, yes? Anyways, here is how the production works!

Step One

First, we put the snow and milk into the ice cream mixer. Next, we turn it on to mix the milk and snow together.

Step Two

After letting it mix for five minutes, we but in the chopped up Mint KitKat pieces. We then mix those in for another 5 minutes.

Step Three

We then put it into the cardboard carton, that is specially insulated, which helps keep it cold during distribution! Lastly, we send it out and the consumer will buy it, then they eat it!
