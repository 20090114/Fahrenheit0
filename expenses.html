Expenses

Here you will see how much it costs for us to produce it and how much the suggested retail price is.

Production Cost

The production cost is very low. This helps consumers get the great deals they want, for a great product. We spend $2.00 on packaging per carton, $1.00 on the ingredints., and $3.00 for distribution and advertising. This comes to a total of $6.00 per carton. This may seem like a lot, but each carton is one gallon, which means, you can use the carton multiple times, before it runs out, since each student gets way less than a gallon.

Suggested Retail Price

We suggest the retail price to be $1.50 per cup of ice cream. There should be three scoops of ice cream to compensate for the price, so that students get the best deal!
